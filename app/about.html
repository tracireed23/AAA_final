A.A.A

Animal Adoption Agency!

We are a non-profit organization that has been helping families find their new pets for over 10 years! Started back in 2005, when the Oliver family decided to dedicate their lives to taking care of and helping cats and dogs find loving homes. Mrs Oliver has always had a strong connections with cats and has always dreamed of being surrounded by cats. She has Mr Oliver, while he does have a soft spot for felines, he loves his big dogs! Together they also own a cat sanctuary where they take the cats who are sick, have special needs or are just old and give them a beautiful place to relax and be loved. They also provide free spay and neuter to those who qualify. Contact us to learn more.
